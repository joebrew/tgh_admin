# Topic specific readings  

For this course you will be asked to read 10-15 articles in your topic area before the course starts. 

When searching for topic specific literature, consider the following guiding questions. 

What is known about this problem? 
Where is it a problem, and for whom? 
Why is it a problem? 
What has been done about this problem?
